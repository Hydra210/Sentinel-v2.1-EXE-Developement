Capabilities

Everything your moderation team needs.

Sentinel was built from the ground up for Roblox asset review. Every feature is purpose-built for moderators who need control, speed, and audit-ready logs.

Asset Review Queue

A fast, keyboard-friendly queue for reviewing flagged assets. Bulk actions let you process hundreds of items without context switching.

Configurable Rules

Define auto-flag policies and action rules based on asset metadata, category, or custom criteria. No manual review required for known patterns.

Full Audit Trail

Every moderation action is logged with timestamps, moderator identity, and outcome. Searchable, exportable, and immutable.

Team Management

Invite moderators with role-based access. Admins control what each team member can view, action, and configure.

Roblox Integration

Cookie-based Roblox authentication lets Sentinel act on assets directly — no manual API setup required.

Live Moderation Stats

Real-time dashboard showing queue depth, team throughput, and flag rate trends. Know exactly how your team is performing.
