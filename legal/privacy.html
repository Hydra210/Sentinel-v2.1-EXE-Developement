Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

EXE Development takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your EXE account and Sentinel data.

Data We Collect

We collect your email address, username, and payment information when you create an EXE account. Sentinel additionally logs moderation actions, asset IDs reviewed, and moderator activity for audit purposes.

How We Use It

Account data is used to authenticate you and manage your Sentinel plan. Moderation logs are retained according to your plan's retention period and are accessible only to your team.

Third Parties

We do not sell your data. Payment processing is handled by a third-party provider. Roblox API access is governed by Roblox's own terms; we only use it within your authorized scope.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your EXE account and associated Sentinel data at any time via the contact page.
